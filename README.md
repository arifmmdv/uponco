# Uponco

Uponco is designed for businesses and individuals offering services to create appointment pages and manage reservations. The platform securely stores customer information to enable future connectivity and relationship management. Upcoming features will include payment processing integration.

## Features

### 📊 Dashboard
A centralized hub for business insights and management.

### 📅 Appointment Management
-   **Interactive Calendar**: Offering day, week, month, and list views.
-   **Booking System**: Streamlined process for creating and managing appointments.

### 🏢 Company Management
-   **Multi-Location Support**: Manage multiple business locations from a single interface.
-   **Service Management**: Define and categorize services offered at different locations.
-   **Staff & Roles**: Manage team members with role-based access control.
-   **Branding**: Customize company branding and settings.

### 👥 Customer Management
-   **Client Database**: Maintain a detailed record of customer information and history.
